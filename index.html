Welcome to Prototypebeta

The fastest way to prototype a web app. Drop this in your document root and go.

Philosophy

You have an hour to put up a demo site on a domain you own. You don't have a prebuilt server ready. You may be on shared hosting. You may not have command line access. This site is just a prototype or a demo, you are not in a position to make long term architectural desisions. The site is relativley simple. You may or my not have any experience making web sites.

What is the fastest way to get a webiste out there that requires the least pre-planning and the smallest learning curve?

Prototypebeta

Features:

  • Basic routing with Toro.
  • PHP native templating.
  • Bare bones database functionality.
  • Requies no configuration or installation: just drop it in your document root and GO!
